INTRODUCTION
Welcome, to the world of forsaken.


Here, there are two, and the only two kinds of people, the survivours, and the killers. By only the name, their goals are clear.


Survivours
-Their goal is to survive and help anyone in need using their abilities.In other words, they are the good people who use thier abilities for good.

Killers
-Their goal is to chase those very survivours to their demise.In other words, they are the bad people who use their abilities for bad purposes.

But they, the killers, weren't always the killers. It was because of him.He manipulated them. Its wrong to call them killers, they should be called the victims.
But the thing is, who is he?
I'll leave that for you to figure out.

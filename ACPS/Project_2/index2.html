ST. Vincent's Convent Sr. Sec. School

Brief History

'St. Vincent's Convent Sr. Sec. School was founded in the year 1974 by Rev. Monsignor Jacob Vadakeveetil, who was the Apostolic Administrator of the Diocese of Balasore at the time. It is a recognized unaided Christian Minority Institution under the religious jurisdiction of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Balasore and its chairperson is Rt. Rev. Simon Kaipuram, the Bishop of Balasore. The school is administered by the Sisters of the Congregation of the Mother of Carmel. Inspired by St. Kuriakose Elias Chavara a great educator, social reformer and the founder of the Congregation of the Mother of Carmel, the sisters offer their dedicated and selfless service for the holistic development of the students and the people in the vicinity.'

'From it's humble origin with 80 students, the school has grown to a full - fledged institution with more than 2000+ students! Today it is regarded as the premier institution of Balasore providing quality education to the children of this locality. Affiliated to the council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations New Delhi (ICSE, ISC Board: OR004)¬ù prepares its students for the ICSE Examination at X level and ISC Examination at +2 level.
The school also pays a lot of respect to their Patron Saint, St. Vincent De Paul.'


' The school has acquired a unique system of motivation and self-discipline and is well known for a sound character formation and quest for excellence. The school has a legacy of excellence in curricular and co-curricular fields and is marching forward achieving accolades and winning laurels in various spheres.'

Enrollment

There are currently about 2100+ students enrolled and counting.If you wish to, you too can become part of the SVCS family right now!

Admissions are currently open for Nursery to STD VIII and XI, year 2025-26.


Available Streams and Board info

Before reaching STD IX, students of class VIII are presented with two choices - one to choose Enviormental Applications (ENA), the other to choose Computer Application. (CA)

Including Arts and Science, this year, the school has added another stream Commerce.

The school follows/ is affiliated with the Council for Indian Certificate School Examination, New Delhi (OR004)


Aims and Objectives

- To equip students with a sound value system to live as good human beings and to impart substantial knowledge and skills to achieve excellence in diverse fields.
- To motivate students to make learning a lifelong process, to strive for self-improvement, and self-transcendence.
- To promote peace and harmony and the spirit of brotherhood transcending religion, linguistic and regional barriers.
- To instil love of motherland and promote appreciation for Indian culture and heritage.


Motto, Vision and Mission

MOTTO

"LOVE, DEDICATION, SERVICE" is our school motto.

VISION

MISSION

We the members of St. Vincent Education Family, animated by our guru Jesus Christ, the teacher par excellence, commit ourselves to contemplation, competence and character formation so as to form efficient, effective, empowered, enriched and dedicated Vincentians who ceaselessly work for the progress of humanity.

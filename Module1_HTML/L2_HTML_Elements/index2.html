There are 9 tags for fonts.

b, strong, i, em, mark, small, del, sub, and sup.

b tag is for bold.
Strong is also for bold.
i for Italics
em is for emphasis (similar to italics)
mark tag to highlight
small tag to make the text comparatively smaller.
del tag to cut a line
sub tag to put a text comparatively lower.
sup tag to put a text comparativelyhigher.
